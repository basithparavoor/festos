Six Portals. One Database.

No more paper trails, no more WhatsApp groups. Every role in your festival gets a dedicated, highly-secured portal that speaks directly to the master cloud.

Master Admin

The core. Create categories, design competitions, register bulk participants via CSV, generate QR ID cards, and manage cross-portal permissions.

  • Bulk CSV Upload & Export
  • Participant ID Generation
  • Global Fest Branding Sync

Team Manager Hub

Give power back to the teams. Managers log in securely to self-assign their roster to competitions, strictly governed by Admin-defined participation limits.

  • Self-Service Roster Allocation
  • Hard-Coded Limit Protections
  • Live Enrollment Tracking

Stage Controller

Hardware-integrated flow control. Controllers scan participant ID cards using device cameras to log attendance and push real-time status updates.

  • Camera-Based QR Scanning
  • One-Click Status Pushing
  • Live Chest-Number Assignment

Digital Valuation

Judges enter marks directly into their secure portal. The system automatically validates against max-marks and averages scores across multiple judges instantly.

  • Automated Average Calculation
  • Max-Mark Protections
  • "Direct Valuation" Admin Bypass

Announcer Deck

The live pulse of the fest. Announcers view a constantly updating feed of exactly who is on stage, who is waiting, and immediate final results.

  • Auto-Refreshing Queue List

  • Live Result Broadcasting
